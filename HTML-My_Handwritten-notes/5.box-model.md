## 🧩 What is the Box Model?

- Every HTML element is considered a box in CSS.
- The Box Model describes how width, height, padding, border, and margin of an element are calculated.



+-------------------------+
|       Margin            |  ← space outside the element
+-------------------------+
|       Border            |  ← border around the padding + content
+-------------------------+
|       Padding           |  ← space between content and border
+-------------------------+
|       Content           |  ← text, image, or inner content
+-------------------------+
